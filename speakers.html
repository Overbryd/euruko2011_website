  • Federico Brubacher

    The Expression Problem in Ruby: A focus on Traits.

    Federico Brubacher is a Rails engineer from Uruguay, a language lover and a proponent of going where no man has gone before. He has many contributions to open source and at Cubox he helps mostly startups to archive their objectives. This talk will introduce you on what the expression problem is, and why it is relevant in Ruby. We then will explain which alternatives are idiomatic in Ruby and analyze pros and cons of each of them. We will advocate for Traits which are basically local mixins.
